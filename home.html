Welcome to IEEE Women in Engineering

We are a global network of professionals dedicated to promoting women engineers and scientists, and inspiring girls around the world to follow their academic interests to a career in engineering.

Our Mission

Our mission is to inspire, engage, and advance women in technical disciplines globally. We provide opportunities for women to connect, collaborate, and grow in their careers.
