Leaders for the 2024-2025 Year

Alice Chang - Chair

Alice has been working in the field of computer security for nearly two decades, playing various roles of network analyst, security researcher, and software developer. Some of her interests have included research and development of heuristic approaches to anomalous network behavior, and has more recently been developing network intelligence infrastructure for Talos Intelligence Group, Cisco's threat intelligence research unit. She is a recent graduate of the Computer Information Sciences Masters program at Syracuse University.

Parisa Rafiee - Vice Chair

Parisa is a PhD candidate in Electrical Engineering at The George Washington University. Her research centers on wireless communications and networks, with a focus on information freshness, 5G/6G technologies, and network coding. By leveraging advancements in Artificial Intelligence and data analytics, she develops innovative solutions for challenges in network optimization. She is passionate about mentorship, serving as a Graduate Student Ambassador, and connecting with others who share an enthusiasm for technology and research. Outside of her professional life, she enjoys hiking, biking, socializing, playing musical instruments, and photography, which help recharge and nurture her creativity.

Vathsala Periasamy - Secretary

Vathsala is a passionate Senior Technical Architect with 18 years of experience, dedicated to transforming raw data into meaningful insights and robust applications. With a solid foundation in data engineering principles and extensive experience in leveraging Amazon Web Services (AWS), she specializes in designing, developing, and optimizing data pipelines and cloud-based solutions. Her journey in the tech industry has been defined by a commitment to excellence, a thirst for innovation, and a relentless pursuit toward solving complex data challenges.
